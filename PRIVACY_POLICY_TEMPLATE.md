# Privacy Policy for SparkiFire

**Last Updated:** [INSERT DATE]

## Introduction

SparkiFire ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy
explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. User-Provided Information

**Text Messages**: We collect the text messages you send through the app to provide AI responses.

**Voice Input**: When you use the voice input feature, we temporarily process your voice recordings
to convert them to text. Voice recordings are not permanently stored.

**Images**: When you share images through the app, we process them to provide relevant AI responses.
Images are temporarily processed and not permanently stored on our servers.

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

**Device Information**: We may collect basic device information such as device type, operating
system version, and app version for troubleshooting purposes.

**Usage Data**: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. **Provide AI Responses**: Your messages, voice input, and images are sent to Google's Gemini AI
   service to generate responses.

2. **Improve User Experience**: We use anonymous usage data to understand how the app is used and
   make improvements.

3. **Technical Support**: Device information helps us diagnose and fix technical issues.

## Third-Party Services

### Google Gemini AI

We use Google's Gemini AI API to process your messages, voice input, and images. Your data is sent
to Google's servers for processing. Please review Google's Privacy Policy
at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Important**:

- We do not store your conversations on our servers
- Your data is processed by Google Gemini AI according to their privacy policy
- We do not share your personal information with any other third parties

## Permissions We Request

### Required Permissions

1. **Internet Access (INTERNET)**: Required to communicate with AI services and provide responses.

### Optional Permissions (User-Granted)

2. **Microphone Access (RECORD_AUDIO)**: Used only when you activate voice input. You can use the
   app without granting this permission.

3. **Camera Access (CAMERA)**: Used only when you choose to take photos. You can use the app without
   granting this permission.

4. **Photo Library Access (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)**: Used only when you choose to
   select images from your gallery. You can use the app without granting this permission.

**Note**: You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings.

## Data Storage and Retention

- **Messages**: Not permanently stored by us. Processed in real-time through Google Gemini AI.
- **Voice Recordings**: Immediately converted to text and discarded. Not stored.
- **Images**: Temporarily processed for AI analysis, then discarded. Not permanently stored.
- **API Keys**: Stored securely within the app and never exposed to users or third parties.

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

1. All data transmission uses secure HTTPS connections
2. No personal conversation data is stored on our servers
3. We do not collect personally identifiable information

## Children's Privacy

SparkiFire is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal
information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has
provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access**: Request information about the data we process
2. **Delete**: Request deletion of your data (note: we don't store conversation history)
3. **Opt-Out**: Stop using the app at any time
4. **Revoke Permissions**: Disable camera, microphone, or photo access through device settings

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Data Deletion

Since we do not store your conversation history or personal data on our servers, there is no data
retention. If you wish to stop using the service:

1. Uninstall the app from your device
2. Any data processed through Google Gemini AI is subject to Google's data retention policies

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United
States, where Google's servers are located. By using SparkiFire, you consent to the transfer of your
information to these countries.
