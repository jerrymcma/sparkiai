Introduction

SparkiFire ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("SparkiFire" or the "App").

Key Point: SparkiFire does NOT store your conversations or personal data on our servers. All data is processed in real-time through Google's Gemini AI service and is not retained by us.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide Directly

  • Text Messages: When you send text messages through the app, they are transmitted to Google's Gemini AI API to generate responses. We do not store these messages.
  • Voice Input: When you use voice input, your voice is converted to text using your device's speech recognition. Voice recordings are processed locally on your device and are not stored by us or transmitted to our servers.
  • Images: When you share images through the app, they are sent to Google's Gemini AI API for analysis. We do not permanently store these images.

2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting and improving app performance.
  • Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to understand how the app is used and to make improvements.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your name, email address, or phone number
  • We do NOT collect your location data
  • We do NOT collect your contacts
  • We do NOT track your browsing history
  • We do NOT require account creation or login

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  1. Provide AI Responses: Your messages, voice input (converted to text), and images are sent to Google's Gemini AI service to generate intelligent responses.
  2. Improve User Experience: Anonymous usage data helps us understand how the app is used and identify areas for improvement.
  3. Technical Support: Device information helps us diagnose and fix technical issues.
  4. App Functionality: To enable features like voice input, image analysis, and personality selection.

Third-Party Services

Google Gemini AI

SparkiFire uses Google's Gemini AI API to process your messages, voice input (as text), and images. Your data is transmitted to and processed by Google's servers according to Google's privacy practices.

  • Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Gemini API Terms: https://ai.google.dev/terms
Important: We do not control Google's data practices. Please review Google's privacy policy to understand how they handle your data when using Gemini AI.

Data Shared with Third Parties

  • Google Gemini AI: Text messages, images, and voice-to-text input are sent to Google for AI processing
  • No Other Third Parties: We do not share your information with any other third parties for marketing, advertising, or any other purposes

Permissions We Request

Required Permission

  • Internet Access (INTERNET): Required to communicate with Google's Gemini AI service and provide responses.

Optional Permissions (You Control)

  • Microphone Access (RECORD_AUDIO): Used only when you activate voice input. You can use the app without granting this permission.
  • Camera Access (CAMERA): Used only when you choose to take photos within the app. You can use the app without granting this permission.
  • Photo Library Access (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): Used only when you choose to select images from your gallery. You can use the app without granting this permission.

Note: You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings. The app will continue to function with limited features if you choose not to grant optional permissions.

Data Storage and Retention

  • Messages: Not permanently stored by SparkiFire. Processed in real-time through Google Gemini AI.
  • Voice Recordings: Processed locally on your device for conversion to text, then immediately discarded. Not stored by SparkiFire.
  • Images: Temporarily processed for AI analysis through Google Gemini AI, then discarded. Not permanently stored by SparkiFire.
  • Conversation History: Not stored. Each conversation session is independent.
  • API Keys: Stored securely within the app and never exposed to users or third parties.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission uses secure HTTPS connections with encryption
  • No conversation data is stored on our servers
  • We do not collect personally identifiable information
  • API communications are encrypted in transit
  • The app does not require account creation, reducing data exposure

However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

SparkiFire is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete such information.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request information about the data we process (note: we don't store conversation history)
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data (note: we don't permanently store conversation data)
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the app at any time
  • Revoke Permissions: Disable camera, microphone, or photo access through your device settings
  • Data Portability: Since we don't store your conversations, there is no data to export

Data Deletion

Since SparkiFire does not store your conversation history or personal data on our servers, there is no data retention. If you wish to stop using the service:

  1. Simply uninstall the app from your device
  2. Any data processed through Google Gemini AI is subject to Google's data retention policies (see Google's Privacy Policy)
  3. Clear the app's cache and data through your device settings before uninstalling (optional)
